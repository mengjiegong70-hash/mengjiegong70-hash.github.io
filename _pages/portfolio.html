Spatiotemporal Accessibility to Elderly Care Services

Spatiotemporal Accessibility to Elderly Care Services

This research focuses on the spatial mismatch between elderly care service supply and aging population demand. It explores how elderly care resources can be optimized under different future development scenarios.

  • Theme: Elderly care accessibility and resource allocation
  • Methods: Spatial accessibility modeling, scenario simulation, optimization analysis
  • Study area: Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area
  • Value: Supporting public service planning and aging-friendly urban development
