<strong>Gong, M.</strong>, Chen, P., Pan, J., et al.  Toward Sustainable Urban Governance: Measuring Economic Vitality via Mobility and Place-Based Commercial Ratings.  
This study proposes a data-driven framework integrating human mobility and place-based commercial data to measure urban economic vitality. By combining static and dynamic indicators, the study captures spatial heterogeneity and diurnal dynamics, offering insights for more targeted and evidence-based urban governance.
